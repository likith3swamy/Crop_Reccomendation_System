Organic Fertilizer Preparation

This section provides a detailed method to prepare organic fertilizer for various crops, ensuring sustainable and eco-friendly farming practices.

Ingredients

Cow dung: 5 kg
Cow urine: 3 liters
Cow milk: 2 liters
Cow curd: 2 liters
Cow ghee: 1 kg
Yellow bananas: 1 dozen
Tender coconut water: 3 liters
Sugarcane juice: 3 liters

Steps to Prepare Organic Fertilizer

1. Mix cow dung with water and let it sit for a few hours.
2. Add cow urine, milk, curd, ghee, bananas, coconut water, and sugarcane juice.
3. Cover and ferment for 21 days, stirring twice daily.
4. Strain the mixture using a cotton cloth.
5. Dilute with water (30:1) to use as a liquid fertilizer.
6. Apply as foliar spray or soil drench during the growing season.

Incorporating organic fertilizer can significantly improve crop yield and quality, promoting sustainable agricultural practices.
